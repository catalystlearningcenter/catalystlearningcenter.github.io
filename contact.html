Get Involved!
Currently, this project is primarily a volunteer effort. Our vision includes growing our volunteer corps & possibly hiring "pre-career" tutors so that we can expand to other libraries and help more students. As a young organization, we're looking for lots of help. If you want to get involved as a tutor or a charitable donor, please contact us for more information.
