Overview
Q: Mission: What's the basic idea?

Catalyst is a group of tutors who operate a drop-in math homework help program at Hosmer Community Library. Typically, we have 1 or 2 students working with a single tutor.

Q: What math topics can Catalyst tutors assist students with?

Catalyst math tutors are knowledgeable in math topics commonly taught at the middle school, junior high, and senior high school levels. Since our inception, we have helped students withâ€¦

  • Pre-Algebra/Basic Math Skills
  • Algebra 1
  • Geometry
  • Algebra 2
  • Trigonometry
  • Calculus
  • Probability & Statistics

Q: What should I bring to a tutoring session?

While the Catalyst tutors have some basic materials (e.g. pencils, paper, rulers, non-graphing calculators) at their sessions, students should also come prepared with the following items to help the tutors determine the best way to assist them:

  • Math assignment
  • Math textbook
  • Math notebook with related notes from class
  • Special math tools needed for assignment (e.g. protractor, compass, graphing calculator)
